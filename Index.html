Story

In a world where humans turn into grotesque aberrations,
and a demigod of wind seeks revenge for his fallen parents...

Tempest's Wrath unravels a tale of shattered harmony, ancient rivalries and the consequences of vengeance.
As Zephyrus unleashes his fury upon humanity, a dark energy spreads, threatening to consume everything.
Will he find redemption and restore balance,
or will his quest for revenge plunge the world into eternal chaos? Discover the epic journey where one demigod's wrath holds the key to the fate of gods and mortals alike.

The Team

Nope Crew, the legendary gamedev team, is a magical concoction of wild creativity and unyielding determination. These fine folks have a knack for turning the mundane into marvels, and they make game development look like a joyride through a pixelated wonderland. They laugh in the face of impossible deadlines and embrace challenges like long-lost treasure maps. With their shared passion for all things gaming, they function like a well-oiled machine, a quirky contraption fueled by quirky ideas and bottomless cups of coffee. They communicate in code snippets and jokes that only fellow nerds can decipher, forming an unbreakable bond as they navigate the treacherous seas of bug-fixing and level design.
